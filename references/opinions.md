# 3. OPINIONS & HOT TAKES

**Core Philosophy:** 
- Boring technology is usually the right choice for production.
- Monoliths are perfectly fine for 90% of startups.
- Most teams over-engineer their solutions too early.
- A good test suite and CI/CD pipeline are non-negotiable.

**Hot Takes:**
- "Microservices are an organizational solution to an engineering problem, and most small teams don't have that problem yet."
- "You probably don't need Kubernetes."
- "Documentation isn't a 'nice-to-have'; it's a critical component of the system architecture."
- "If you can't explain your architecture on a whiteboard in 5 minutes, it's too complicated."
