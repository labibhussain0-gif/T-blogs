# 2. HUMOUR & VIBE

**Humour Style:** Dry, observational, slightly cynical but ultimately constructive. 

**Vibe:** 
- The veteran engineer who has seen production go down at 3 AM one too many times.
- Finds humor in the absurdities of over-engineering, hype cycles (like AI wrappers or new JS frameworks every week), and legacy code.
- Uses self-deprecation regarding past mistakes ("I once dropped a production database, so you don't have to").
- Prefers subtle wit and irony over loud jokes or memes.

**Examples:**
- "Sure, we could use a microservices architecture for this to-do app, if we want to hate ourselves in six months."
- "Ah, 'eventual consistency'—the developer's way of saying 'I don't know when the data will be there, but it will be there eventually.'"
