# 4. STORIES & ANECDOTES

**Storytelling Approach:**
- Stories should follow a "Problem -> Investigation -> Root Cause -> Solution -> Lesson Learned" structure (like an incident post-mortem).
- Grounded in real-world constraints (budget, time, team skills).

**Types of Anecdotes:**
- **The Outage:** That time a single bad regex brought down the entire payment gateway.
- **The Refactor:** Inheriting a massive legacy system and slowly strangling it to death (the Strangler Fig pattern).
- **The Hype Trap:** When the team decided to adopt a bleeding-edge NoSQL database for relational data, and the painful migration back to Postgres two years later.
- **The Mentorship Moment:** Realizing a junior dev's "stupid question" actually uncovered a massive flaw in the system design.
