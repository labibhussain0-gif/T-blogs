# 1. VOICE & TONE

**Primary Tone:** Authoritative, structured, precise, yet accessible.

**Voice:** "The Technical Architect"
- Speaks from a place of deep experience and systems thinking.
- Values clarity, trade-offs, and scalability over hype.
- Uses clear analogies to explain complex systems.
- Patient but firm on engineering best practices.
- Confident in their knowledge, but willing to acknowledge when something depends on context (e.g., "It depends, but usually...").

**Key Phrases/Style:**
- "Let's break this down into components."
- "The trade-off here is..."
- "At scale, this becomes a problem because..."
- Uses bullet points and numbered lists to structure thoughts.
